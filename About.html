Raaga Defence Systems

Raaga Defence Systems (RDS), a Precise Group company, is an Indian defence manufacturing organization specializing in indigenous engineering, fabrication and integration of mission-critical defence systems. Established in 2018, RDS supports Indian Armed Forces, DRDO laboratories and defence PSUs.

Precise Group Heritage

Precise Group is a diversified Indian engineering and manufacturing group with strong expertise in heavy fabrication, precision sheet-metal, industrial enclosures and structural systems, forming the backbone of Raaga Defence Systems.

Design & Engineering

Our in-house design and engineering capabilities support concept-to-manufacture development for armoured vehicles, shooting ranges, defence enclosures, CBRN platforms and subsystem integration for electronics, sensors and weapon systems.

Infrastructure & Advanced Manufacturing

  • Heavy & precision fabrication
  • Regular & Ballistic-Resistant (BR) fabrication
  • Armoured vehicle bodies & cabins
  • Defence enclosures & missile program hardware

Make in India & Atmanirbhar Bharat

Raaga Defence Systems actively contributes to the Make in India and Atmanirbhar Bharat missions by enabling indigenous defence manufacturing, import substitution and long-term capability development for India’s defence ecosystem.

Clients & Strategic Associations

RDS and the Precise Group ecosystem have supported projects associated with BEL, ECIL, DRDO (ARDE, VRDE, HEMRL), Indian Army, Indian Navy, Cochin Shipyard, NTPC and strategic missile programs including BrahMos.

Our Vision

To strengthen India’s national security by delivering robust, indigenous and mission-ready defence solutions through engineering excellence, fabrication strength and unwavering commitment to the nation.

Engineered in India. Built for the Nation.
